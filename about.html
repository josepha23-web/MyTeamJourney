NBA 2k History

NBA 2k video game series is an annual video game devloped by Visual Concepts and published by 2k. The first game was released in 1999 with NBA 2k and the latest installment is NBA 2k26. There are different game modes in 2k, which are MyCareer, MyTeam, MyNBA, PlayNow and MyWNBA. Kobe Bryant has been on the cover art the most with 6 times with his last time being for 2k24. This years cover athletes are Shai Gilgeous-Alexander, Angel Reese on the Leave No Doubt Edition and Carmelo Anthony on the Superstar Edition.

MyTeam

The MyTeam mode was first introduced in NBA 2k13 version of the game. The main idea for this game mode is for players to collect their favourite players in card form and battle against either the CPU or against online opponents. The main way to get these cards is to open packs that are released weekly or try their luck with the auction house and purchase cards directly from other players with the in-game currency MT Coins. There are different game modes within MyTeam. The two main modes are Domination and Triple Threat Online. Domination mode is a staple in the mode, where players get their best squad together to complete challenges and compete against the CPU teams which can range from current day squads or historic teams. The main online mode is Triple Threat Online, where players compete against one another in either a 1v1, 2v2, or 3v3.

MyTeam Journey

I’ve been playing the NBA 2k series since NBA 2k12 and started playing the MyTeam mode when it was released. In this year’s release, the mode started off a bit slow due to the fact there wasn’t much to grind for. Although that did change with the release of a Pink Diamond Jack Sikma card that has great stats that will make usable until December. I started the grind for him playing the Breakout game mode where you must clear a little map of different game modes (Triple Threat Offline, Clutch Time and Full line-up). To unlock the card, you must complete the breakout board 15 different times and collect Breakout Reward cards that were then exchanged for the Season 1 Reward card which then let you choose to pick three different players, Jack Sikma, Glenn Rice and Jason Kidd. Because I mainly play as a centre on Triple Threat Online with friends, I picked Jack Sikma and haven’t looked back since.

Jack Sikma

Jack Sikma is an NBA champion and was a part of the 2019 Naismith Basketball Hall of Fame class. He was drafted by the Seattle Supersonics with the 8th overall pick. He was mainly known for his career at the Seattle SuperSonics where he won an NBA championship in his second year in 1979, and having a stint with the Milwaukee Bucks until 1991 when he retired. As well as being an NBA champion, he was an NBA All-Star seven times, between 1979 to 1985 and a selected in the NBA All-Defensive Second Team in 1982. Throughout his career he averaged 15.6 points on 46% shooting, 9.8 rebounds and 3.2 assists. His best year stat wise was in the 1981-1982 season where he averaged 19.6 points, 12.7 rebounds, 3.4 assists and a block a game. This same year he was voted 9th in the MVP voting.
